How to Play

🎯 Objective

Destroy the alien beehive and eliminate all bees before they deplete your health!

🎮 Keyboard Controls

WASD - Move ship
Space - Fire weapons
V - Use special weapon
B - Heavy weapon
C - Defensive tech
Shift - Cycle special

You can remap keys from Custom Game on the main menu.

📱 Touch Controls

🕹️ Left joystick - Move ship
🔴 FIRE button - Shoot (hold)
🔵 V button - Special weapon
🟣 ⟳ button - Cycle weapon
🟠 B button - Heavy weapon
🟢 C button - Defensive tech

⚡ Special Weapons

❄️ Freeze - Slows enemies
⚡ Electric - Area damage
🌀 Warp - Teleport forward

💥 Heavy Ordnance

🌀 Singularity - Gravity vortex
⚡ Railgun - Piercing beam
💥 Shockwave - Stuns all

🛡️ Defensive Tech

🛡️ Shield - Energy barrier
🚀 Missiles - Homing
👻 Cloak - Invisibility

⚠️ Tips

  • Hive is protected for 10 seconds at start
  • Don't stay still - hunters will find you!
  • Destroy honey cells for bonus points
